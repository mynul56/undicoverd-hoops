# UNDISCOVERED HOOPS: GO-TO-MARKET & 90-DAY LAUNCH SPRINT
**Version:** 1.0.0 (Execution Lock)

This document defines the exact execution strategy for bootstrapping the Undiscovered Hoops marketplace. The core strategic mandate is: **Supply (Players) must be established before monetizing Demand (Coaches).**

---

## 1. CORE GTM STRATEGY

Undiscovered Hoops faces the classic "cold start" marketplace problem. Our strategy solves this by utilizing **User-Generated Content (Reels) as the primary acquisition engine.**
- Players generate Reels.
- Reels drive discovery and external organic traffic.
- High-quality talent pools attract Coaches.
- Coaches convert to paid subscribers for access and efficiency.

---

## 2. ACQUISITION CHANNELS

### Channel 1: Campuses + AAU Teams (Highest ROI)
- **Target:** High schools, AAU basketball circuits, and summer training camps.
- **Tactic:** Direct, boots-on-the-ground outreach. Onboarding entire teams at once through demo sessions. 

### Channel 2: Social Media Viral Loop
- **Target:** TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts.
- **Tactic:** Aggressive cross-posting of player highlight reels. Content strategy focuses on "Hidden Talent Discovered" narratives and "Coach Reaction" videos. All shared videos include a distinct Undiscovered Hoops watermark and CTA.

### Channel 3: Influencer Ecosystem
- **Target:** Basketball trainers, micro-influencer athletes, and established recruiting analysts.
- **Tactic:** Partnership deals to have influencers review player profiles on the app, driving their audiences to sign up to be reviewed.

### Channel 4: Event Partnerships
- **Target:** AAU Event organizers and Showcase camps.
- **Tactic:** B2B partnerships where event registration includes a mandatory Undiscovered Hoops profile creation.

---

## 3. TWO-SIDED ACQUISITION STRATEGY

### Player Acquisition (The Supply)
- **Strategy:** Always 100% Free access for core features.
- **Gamification:** Highlighting "Profile Views", "Trending Rankings", and "Coach Interest Alerts" to tap into social validation and athletic ego.
- **Viral Trigger:** *"A D1 Coach just viewed your profile"* (Push Notification).

### Coach Acquisition (The Demand & Revenue)
- **Value Proposition:** Direct access to verified, hidden talent with advanced filtering (height, stats, academics) saving hundreds of hours in travel and scouting.
- **Onboarding Flow:** 
  1. Invited via email/event list.
  2. 14-Day Free Trial (Full Access).
  3. Search & Match Interaction.
  4. Trial Expiration → Paywall Conversion.
- **Conversion Strategy:** Post-trial, Coaches can only see limited profiles and are blocked from direct messaging until they subscribe to **Coach Pro**.

---

## 4. THE 90-DAY EXECUTION SPRINT

### Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1 - 30)
*Goal: Fix the leaky bucket and establish the initial supply pool.*
- **Target:** 1,000 Players | 100 Coaches.
- **Actions:**
  - Execute manual, campus-level onboarding for the first 1,000 players.
  - Host 1-on-1 demo sessions with regional coaches.
  - Fix all UX/UI friction points in the upload and onboarding flow.

### Phase 2: Growth (Days 31 - 60)
*Goal: Ignite the viral loop and validate willingness to pay.*
- **Target:** 10,000 Players | 500 Coaches.
- **Actions:**
  - Launch influencer campaigns.
  - Activate event partnerships at regional tournaments.
  - Turn on the Paywall for the Phase 1 cohort of Coaches (First MRR generated).

### Phase 3: Scale (Days 61 - 90)
*Goal: Pour fuel on the fire and optimize unit economics.*
- **Target:** 50,000 Players | 2,000 Coaches.
- **Actions:**
  - Begin scaling paid user acquisition (Facebook/TikTok Ads targeted at athletes).
  - Heavily optimize the Recommendation Engine based on Phase 2 swipe data.
  - A/B test subscription pricing tiers to maximize Coach conversion rates.

---

## 5. FUNNEL DESIGN & METRICS

**The Core Funnel:**
`App Install → Profile Creation → Upload Reel → Accumulate Views → Coach Interaction → Match/Chat → Coach Subscription Conversion`

### Primary Tracking KPIs
1. **DAU / MAU Ratio:** Measures habit-forming usage.
2. **Reel Watch Time:** Measures content quality and feed algorithm accuracy.
3. **Match Rate:** % of swipes that result in a mutual match.
4. **Subscription Conversion Rate:** % of Coaches crossing the paywall.
5. **Day-7 & Day-30 Retention:** Are players coming back?

---

## 6. RISK FACTORS & MITIGATION

- **Risk (The Empty Room):** Players join but there are no Coaches to recruit them.
  - **Mitigation:** Manually recruit and subsidize a baseline of 100 active coaches before any mass-marketing push.
- **Risk (Low Engagement):** Poor feed ranking leads to disengagement.
  - **Mitigation:** Aggressive monitoring of Watch Time metrics. If watch time drops, manually curate the feed algorithm until AI weighting stabilizes.
- **Risk (Failed Monetization):** Coaches use the app but refuse to pay.
  - **Mitigation:** Ensure the core value (Messaging/Contacting) is strictly gated. If they want the player, they must pay the toll.
